# BriefLoop Red Lines

Read this when a requested shortcut might bypass the control plane.

- Do not edit frozen artifacts in place.
- Do not edit control files to make a command pass.
- Do not bypass stage completion, gates, repair transactions, or archives.
- Do not auto-approve memory.
- Do not treat search snippets, source plans, or model summaries as evidence.
- Do not turn an input limitation into a fake repair.
- Do not rehabilitate contaminated evidence by narrative.
- Do not claim truth proof, hallucination elimination, autonomous repair,
  semantic support proof, or ready-to-send output unless implemented artifacts
  support that exact claim.
- Do not publish private facts, private golden cases, or internal planning as
  public mechanism evidence.

If a shortcut seems necessary, stop and ask for a deterministic transaction,
human review, fresh workspace, or explicit public-safe boundary.
